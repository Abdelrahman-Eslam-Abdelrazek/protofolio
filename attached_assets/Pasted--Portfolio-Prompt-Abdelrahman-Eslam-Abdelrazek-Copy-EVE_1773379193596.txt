## 🎨 DESIGN DIRECTION — THIS IS THE MOST IMPORTANT PART

### Theme: "Editorial Depth" — White base, ink black, with 3D presence

**Color Palette:**
```
--white: #ffffff
--off-white: #f5f5f3
--paper: #fafaf8
--ink: #0a0a0a
--ink-soft: #1a1a1a
--ink-muted: #555555
--ink-faint: #999999
--accent: #0a0a0a
--border: #e0e0dc
--shadow-soft: rgba(0,0,0,0.06)
--shadow-deep: rgba(0,0,0,0.18)
```

**NO colors** — no blue, no cyan, no gold, no gradients. Pure black ink on white paper. The drama comes from **depth, shadows, and 3D motion** — not color.

### Typography
Import from Google Fonts:
- **"Bebas Neue"** — hero name only, massive, condensed, ultra-bold
- **"Playfair Display"** — section headings, elegant serif
- **"DM Sans"** — body text, clean and modern
- **"JetBrains Mono"** — code snippets, tech badges

### 3D & Animation Style — THIS IS CRITICAL
The entire portfolio must feel like it exists in **physical space**. Use CSS `perspective`, `transform: translateZ()`, `rotateX()`, `rotateY()` everywhere.

Specific effects required:

1. **Hero section:** The name "ABDELRAHMAN ESLAM" splits into two layers — front layer (solid black) and back layer (gray, slightly offset) creating a real 3D shadow/depth effect. On mouse move, the layers shift slightly with parallax, as if the text physically responds to where you look.

2. **Scroll reveal:** Every section card/element rises from below AND tilts forward slightly as it enters view — like cards being dealt from a deck toward the viewer. Use `transform: perspective(1000px) rotateX(15deg) translateY(60px)` as start state, animate to `rotateX(0deg) translateY(0px)`.

3. **Project cards:** On hover, the card lifts with `translateZ(20px)` and a deep shadow appears beneath it — like picking up a physical card off a table. The card slightly rotates toward your cursor (JS mouse tracking per card, max ±8deg rotation).

4. **Navbar:** Frosted glass with slight shadow. On scroll, a thin black bottom border appears with a subtle drop shadow.

5. **Skill badges:** On hover, each badge pops forward with `translateZ(8px) scale(1.05)` — like pressing a raised button.

6. **Page load:** Staggered entrance — navbar slides down, hero text slams in with a slight overshoot bounce (CSS spring easing), then subtitle fades in, then CTA buttons rise up. Total duration: 1.2 seconds.

7. **Typing animation** on hero subtitle, cycling through:
   - "Full-Stack Developer"
   - "Web Security Specialist"  
   - "Building AI Security Tools"

8. **Custom cursor:** A small solid black circle (12px) that follows the mouse with slight lag (lerp smoothing). On hovering links/buttons, it scales up to 40px and becomes a ring (border only, transparent fill).
